# Rule 02 — Bench Rotation

## When benching applies

If the number of signups exceeds the available raid spots for a given night, excess players are benched.

- **Karazhan night:** 30 spots (3 x 10). Signups beyond 30 are benched.
- **Gruul + Magtheridon night:** 25 spots. Signups beyond 25 are benched.

## Fairness requirement

Bench assignments must rotate so that **every non-officer player is benched a similar number of times per raid location** over the course of multiple raid nights. Bench counts are tracked **separately** for Karazhan and for Gruul+Magtheridon — a player's Karazhan bench count and their Gruul+Mag bench count are independent.

When deciding who to bench, compare players' bench counts **for the specific raid location being planned**. A player who has been benched 0 times from Gruul+Mag should sit before a player who has been benched 1 time from Gruul+Mag, regardless of how many times either has been benched from Karazhan (and vice versa).

Previous bench history (tracked in prior sets) must be consulted before deciding who sits.

## Officer exemption

The following players are **exempt from being benched** (they always raid):

- Mirohl
- Jar
- Glaivemaster Baebay
- Kres/Dissi
- Greg (Ucannotpass)

## Bench tracking

Each generated set must record who was benched, so that subsequent sets can ensure fair rotation.
