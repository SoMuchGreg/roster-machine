# Rule 01 — Raid Composition Requirements

## Karazhan (10-man)

Each Karazhan raid team must contain exactly:

| Role    | Count |
|---------|-------|
| Tank    | 2     |
| Healer  | 2     |
| DPS     | 6     |
| **Total** | **10** |

Three Karazhan raids are formed per Karazhan night (30 players total if full).

## Gruul + Magtheridon (25-man)

The single 25-man raid must contain exactly:

| Role    | Count |
|---------|-------|
| Tank    | 2     |
| Healer  | 6     |
| DPS     | 17    |
| **Total** | **25** |
