# 2026-04-11 — Shaman & Druid DPS: melee vs. ranged distinction

## Change

Rule 04 (Player Specializations) updated to explicitly note that DPS Shamans and DPS Druids must be categorized as either **melee** (Enhancement / Feral) or **ranged/caster** (Elemental / Balance). The signup screenshot spec icon determines which spec they're playing for that raid.

## Reason

The melee vs. ranged distinction matters for raid composition and upcoming 5-man group composition rules. Enhancement and Feral play fundamentally differently from Elemental and Balance.

## Impact

- `rules/04-player-specs.md` — added general rule about melee/ranged DPS for Shamans and Druids
- The DPS Shamans (Lynelen, Tymoti, Ebonybolt, Pergatori) and DPS Druid (Yxanb) still have unknown specific specs — their spec icon must be checked each raid
