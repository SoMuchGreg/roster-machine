# 2026-04-19 — Canonical name / Character / Main / Alt vocabulary

## Change
Added four player-identity terms to `config/project.md` → "Terminology": **Canonical name** (incl. the composite-name rule — composite whenever a name is commonly used to refer to the player — and the signup collapse behavior), **Character**, **Main**, and **Alt**. ## Reason
Both terms were used throughout the project (multiple rule files, the file-operations manual, derived files) without a canonical definition, and with inconsistent phrasing ("canonical name", "canonical Player", "canonical player names"). The composite-name convention (`Kres/Dissi`, `Marino-Varthier`, `Glaivemaster Baebay`) also lacked a stated rule. Formalizing the vocabulary in the single Terminology table keeps all usages pointing at one source and prevents future drift.
