## Change

Added a **tank-surplus flex** rule to `rules/01-raid-compositions.md` under General principles: when more tanks sign up than the raid's composition target calls for, a dual-spec tank is offered a swap to their offspec before being auto-benched, mirroring the existing dual-spec flex rule for role shortages. The rule also defers to the raid leader's explicit bench intent when one is signaled.

## Reason

Discovered during the 2026-04-15 Gruul+Mag roster: a non-core tank signed up in the Tank column, but with only 3 tank slots and 3 core tanks already signing up, the planner needed a mechanism to either flex the extra tank to DPS/Healer or bench them — previously undocumented.
