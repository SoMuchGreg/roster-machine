# 2026-04-12 — Karazhan Resto Druid cap softened

## Change
`rules/01-raid-compositions.md` — the Karazhan "No more than 1 Resto Druid per team" constraint is now a soft rule rather than a hard one.

## Reason
The roster has a large Resto Druid pool. A strict per-team cap can force Resto Druids to bench even when other teams would benefit from a second. Softening lets the planner trade off composition diversity against bench fairness when signups are skewed.
